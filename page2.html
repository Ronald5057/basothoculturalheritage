Basotho Traditional Clothing

The Basotho people are known for their unique and symbolic traditional attire, which reflects their history, values, and way of life. Below are examples of clothing that represent Basotho culture.

Basotho Blanket

A symbol of pride and identity, worn during ceremonies and daily life.

Traditional teenager Cloths

Young Basotho girls wear (a) Sefaha sa letsopa le Thethana ya banana, which in translation are a neckpiece and a skirt.

Learn more

Adulthood

Young men and women go to initiation school where they are taught about life, growth and being a contributing member of society. Young girls get taught how to take care of a household while boys are taught about courage and endurance.
